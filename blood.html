Blood Bank

Saving Lives, One Donation at a Time

At Rotaract, we are committed to saving lives by providing safe and reliable blood products to those in need. Every donation makes a difference, and with your help, we can ensure that no patient goes without the life-saving blood they require.

Our Mission

Our mission is to ensure a safe, sufficient, and timely supply of blood and blood components to hospitals and healthcare providers. We are dedicated to serving our community by collecting, testing, processing, and distributing blood products to those who need them the most.

Why Donate Blood?

  • Save Lives: Every two seconds, someone needs blood. By donating, you are giving someone a second chance at life.
  • Health Benefits: Donating blood can help reduce the risk of heart disease and cancer while keeping your iron levels in check.
  • Community Support: Your donation directly supports your local community, helping friends, neighbors, and others in need.

    Partner with Us

    Organizations, companies, and community groups can partner with us to host blood drives. By working together, we can save more lives and make a larger impact.

    Who Can Donate?

    To donate blood, you must:

    • Be in good health.
    • Be at least 18 years old.
    • Weigh at least 50 kg (110 lbs).
    • Not have donated blood in the last 56 days.
    • Pass a brief health screening on the day of donation.

    Types of Donations

    • Whole Blood Donation: The most common type, where you donate approximately one pint of blood.
    • Platelet Donation: Helps patients undergoing cancer treatments, organ transplants, and surgeries.
    • Double Red Cell Donation: Ideal for those with type O blood, it allows you to donate two units of red cells.
