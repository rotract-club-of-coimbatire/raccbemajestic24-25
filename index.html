Who Are We?

The Rotaract Club of Coimbatore Majestic, sponsored by The Rotary Club of Coimbatore Midtown, was chartered on May 5, 1991. The inaugural President was Rtr. PP Geeta Lakshmi. Over its 33 years, the club has proudly produced three Majestic District Rotaract Representatives for Rotary International District 3201: Rtr. PP Mohammad Nasar, Rtr. PP Vineesh, and Rtr. PP Jimry Henry. The club has consistently served the community and is dedicated to supporting both community initiatives and the professional development of its members.
