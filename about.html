About Majestic

Our History

The Rotaract Club of Coimbatore Majestic, parented by The Rotary Club of Coimbatore Midtown was Chartered on 5th of May in 1991. The Charter President was Rtr.PP.Geeta Lakshmi. Our Club has produced 3 Majestic District Rotaract Representative in the history of Rotary International District 3201 namely Rtr.PP.Mohammad Nasar, Rtr.PP. Vineesh, Rtr.PP.Jimry Henry.

For 33 years and counting, the Rotaract Club of Coimbatore Majestic has faithfully served the community. With a legacy of impactful leadership and continuous support for community initiatives, the club remains committed to the professional growth of its members.

The Club has been active ever since and it is continuously supporting the community and indulged in developing the professional career of the fellow club members.

The Rotaract Club of Coimbatore Comito, the Rotaract Club of PSGCAS and the Rotaract Club of SRCAS are the sister clubs with whom we have maintained a constant healthy bond.

Our Vision

To be a leading force in community service and youth development, creating lasting impact through innovation and collaboration.

Our Mission

To serve the community, empower youth, and foster leadership through impactful projects, collaboration, and personal development.

Our Works

Health camps, literacy drives, environmental initiatives, orphanage support, youth empowerment, cultural events, and collaborations with sister clubs and Rotary.
